About Our Club

Bringing together UT Austin’s swimmers, cyclists, and runners since 2015

Who We Are

The UT Triathlon & Cycling Club is a student‑run organization dedicated to promoting endurance sports on campus. Whether you’re a seasoned triathlete or new to cycling, we welcome athletes of all levels. Our mission is to:

  • Foster a supportive community for training and racing
  • Organize weekly group rides, swims, and runs
  • Provide coaching clinics and skills workshops
  • Host social events to connect members

Our History

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate UT Austin students, the club has grown from a handful of members to a thriving community of over 100 endurance athletes. We’ve competed in local triathlons, hosted campus fundraisers, and partnered with the University Recreation Center for swim times.

What We Offer

Weekly Group Workouts

Structured rides, runs, and swims led by experienced members.

Beginner Clinics

Introductory sessions on bike handling, open‑water swimming, and transition techniques.

Member Spotlights

Learn from veteran triathletes and their training tips.

Social Events

Monthly gatherings, gear swaps, and post‑race celebrations.
