About Us

We’re a student‑run club welcoming athletes of all levels—from first‑timers to age‑group competitors. Our mission is to train together, race together, and build a supportive UT Longhorn community.

  • Weekly group rides, swims & runs
  • Skills clinics and coaching
  • Social events & gear swaps

Membership

What are the benefits of joining the team?

Customized team training, monthly socials, 3–4 races per semester, and a trip to the National Collegiate Triathlon Championship.

What type of experience do I need to join?

Absolutely none! We welcome everyone—from first‑timers to veteran endurance athletes.

Do you do anything besides training?

Yes! Monthly socials, holiday parties, and post‑brick rides to Cobo Bob’s are all part of the fun.

How many members are on the team?

Around 40–50. Last season we sent 11 to nationals; this season we’re aiming for 25+!

Practices

What are practices like?

Social, structured sessions: warm‑up, main workout, cool‑down—built around our season plan.

Do I have to attend every practice?

No—practices are optional so you can balance school, work, and life.

Races

What races do you compete in?

3–4 Conference Championship races each semester, plus 2–3 in Spring for Nationals.

Can I still race on my own?

Absolutely—many members do external sprints and Ironmans alongside us.
