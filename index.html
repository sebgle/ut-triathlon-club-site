About Us

We’re a student‑run club welcoming athletes of all levels—from first‑timers to age‑group competitors. Our mission is to train together, race together, and build a supportive UT Longhorn community.

  • Weekly group rides, swims & runs
  • Skills clinics and coaching
  • Social events & gear swaps
