How to Join

Ready to make it official? Just follow these two simple parts.

Part 1: Membership Packet

  1. Visit UT RecSports Members & Officers
  2. Click “Membership Packet” at the bottom.
  3. Log in with your UT EID.
  4. Click “Start/Continue Membership Packet.”
    (Duplicate if you’ve filled one before.)
  5. Select “Texas Triathlon” as your Sports Club.

Part 2: Club Dues

  1. Go back to RecSports Members & Officers
  2. Click “Membership Packet” and log in again.
  3. Click “Online Store” → “Sports Club Deposits.”
  4. Choose “Texas Triathlon” → “Sports Club Dues.”
    (Dues are $200/yr or $125/sem.)
  5. Add to cart & checkout.
  6. Notify Rykhr Grissom so you can join our Slack channels.
